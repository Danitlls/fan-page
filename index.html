Silicon Valley is an American comedy television series created by Mike Judge, John Altschuler and Dave Krinsky. The series focuses on six young men who found a startup company in Silicon Valley. The series premiered on April 6, 2014 on HBO.


Silicon Valley is currently airing its fourth season, which premiered on April 23, 2017. The series has been renewed for a fifth season, which will premiere in 2018.
